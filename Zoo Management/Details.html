Animals

A zoo is also known as a zoological park or zoological garden, it is a place where animals are confined within enclosures for the public to view. There are currently over 100 large animal collections open to the public in this zoo. The animals are well-maintained and cared for by trained professionals in closely-monitored environments under constant surveillance.

Zoo Cells

There are various types of zoo cells in the zoo where animals are kept for your viewing pleasure. In this section, you can find out the location and categorization of every single cell in the zoo. The zoo cells provide all basic amenities for the comfort and daily requirements of the animals such as feeding area, resting area, breeding area, research area and rehabilitation area.

Children's Ticket

Children's ticket is a special type of ticket which is scanned at the entrance where the zoo staff are notified of the presence of children so that they can taken care of and to ensure that the zoo staff are well-prepared to tackle all of their questions as well as assign a tour guide. Children's tickets are made affordable in order to meet the goals of the zoological park to enlighten the younger generations. Unauthorized entry is not allowed so tickets are mandatory.



Adult's ticket

Adult's ticket is the standard type of ticket which scanned at the entrance where the zoo staff are notified of new visitors so that they can arrange the necessary facilities and assign a suitable tour guide to show them around the various sections of the zoo. Food and refreshments are not included in the ticket prices because they are sold separately at the cafeteria. Unauthorized entry is not allowed so tickets are mandatory.



Membership

If you buy our membership package, you become a member of our coveted Wildlife Alliance which has international recognition. It is a program where you learn all of the basics of survival in the wild, taking care of both healthy and injured animals, go on safaris and meet our professioal zookeepers who are going to teach you everything about zookeeping. The membership package is a two-year-long journey into a different world. Apply now!
